Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

Grilled cheese is one of America's simplest foods to make. Despite this, it is one of many's comfort foods. Many people also have different iterations of this classic meal.

Today, we will be learning how to make grilled cheese in its purest forms.

Ingredients

  • American Cheese
  • Whole Wheat Bread Slices
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put one tablespoon of butter on a pan in medium heat.
  2. Put two slices of whole wheat bread on the pan.
  3. Flip the slices in two minutes. Then flip the slices again after another minute.
  4. On one slice, place one slice of American cheese on top of the bread slice. Then put the other slice with no cheese on top of the bread that has the cheese slice.
  5. Squish the now sandwhich and flip it once more in two minutes.
  6. Take the sandwich off the pan and enjoy your beautiful grilled cheese.
